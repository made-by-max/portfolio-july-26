    I built a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) application to perform a semantic search of my HOA's bylaws and local property ordinances so that residents can easily find out what is and is not allowed.

        I used Pinecone as my vector database, OpenAI to create the embeddings, and Anthropic to perform the text generation.
